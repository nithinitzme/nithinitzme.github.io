About

I am an Assistant Research Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Arizona. My work spans quantum error correction and fault-tolerant quantum computation, with contributions to trapping-set analysis in (Q)LDPC codes, soft-syndrome and flipping decoders, and hardware-aware low-latency architectures. I co-lead NSF-funded research on scalable QLDPC decoding and mentor graduate researchers across software and FPGA implementation tracks.
